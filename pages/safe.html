Safety Tips

Simple, practical steps to help you stay safe in public spaces and while travelling.

Plan Your Route

Know your route before leaving. Share your travel details with a trusted friend or family member.

Stay Visible at Night

Avoid dark alleys. Use streetlights, carry a flashlight, or wear reflective clothing for better visibility.

Basic Self-Defense

Learn simple moves to protect yourself. Always carry a whistle or personal safety alarm.

Trust Your Instincts

If you feel uncomfortable or sense danger, move to a safe place immediately and call for help.
