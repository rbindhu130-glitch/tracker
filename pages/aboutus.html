About SafeTracker

Towards a Safer, More Aware Community

Our Story

In many cities, people face harassment, stalking, and unsafe environments while traveling. These experiences often create fear and restrict freedom.

The idea for SafeTracker began with Anu — a working professional in Chennai who travels home alone every night. Dark streets, lack of streetlights, and unsafe surroundings made every journey a challenge. Like Anu, thousands of women, students, and elderly citizens face similar dangers every day.

We wanted to change that. SafeTracker was built to give people a voice, a tool, and a community where help is just a click away.

Our Mission

To create a platform that empowers individuals to report unsafe situations, connect with nearby volunteers, and access reliable safety resources — all in real time.

What We Do

  • Enable users to report incidents and request help instantly.
  • Connect verified volunteers to those in need nearby.
  • Provide safety resources like helplines and police station info.
  • Track and analyze high-risk areas to improve community safety.

Who We Serve

  • People at Risk: Anyone who feels unsafe in public spaces — women, students, elders, and travelers.
  • Volunteers: Verified individuals willing to help and ensure safe journeys for others.

Our Vision

A city where every person — no matter their gender, age, or time of travel — feels safe, supported, and confident to move freely.
